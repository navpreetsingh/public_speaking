  • Sign Up for a Toptal TopTalks Event (Optional)

    TopTalks are a series of TED-style presentations in under 10-minutes, hosted over Zoom, showcasing a well-informed idea on any topic. Your talk could be about a tech demo, an interesting take on a topic, an issue unknown to others, or a project you are proud of. All public speaking levels are welcome. These events are only available to the Toptal Community and is a safe space for speakers to have a platform to test a new idea they have been working on, prepare for an external meetup or conference talk, or simply to practice your public speaking skills while gaining valuable feedback from the room. The audience will comprise of Core Team members and fellow Toptalers who wish to support their community, provide feedback, and listen to the topics.
